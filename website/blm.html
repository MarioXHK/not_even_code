Welcome to the page about this person named

Melba Roy Mouton!!!

She was born in 1929 and died on the 25th of June. 1990.

She was borned in Fairfax Virginia and got a master's degree in mathematics in Howard University.

She was a leader in a group of math people who were also known at the time as "computers" who were monitoring a satelite called Echo 1in the early 1960s. She then became a Head Computer Programmer and then a Program Production Section Chief at the Goddard Space Flight Center. Fer all her effort, she was awarded the Apollo Achievement Award.
